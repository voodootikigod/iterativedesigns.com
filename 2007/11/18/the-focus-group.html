The Focus Group

The Focus Group is a new start-up in the Washington DC area focusing on Information Security evaluation and recommendation. They seeking a branding and website that conveyed their experience and professionalism, all the while catering to the local federal and commercial markets. Iterative Designs developed the logo and branding for the company followed by the web site design. Their site has been constructed on a popular content management system to allow them to extend, grow, or shrink the site as necessary. They are currently in the process of finalizing content for the site.

Services Provided:

  • Website Design
  • Logo and Branding
  • Web Hosting Setup and Configuration
